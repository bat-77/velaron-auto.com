Our Story

Bridging Japan & Ireland

Velaron Auto was founded on a simple observation: the Japanese luxury car market and the Irish market operate almost entirely independently — creating massive arbitrage opportunities for those who know how to bridge them.

We are not a traditional import firm. We are a data-driven sourcing operation with direct auction access in Japan and deep market knowledge in Ireland.

Our team includes partners in Tokyo who inspect vehicles, review auction sheets, and bid on our behalf. In Ireland, we work directly with dealerships who understand the value of exclusive, low-mileage Japanese imports.
