How We Import

From auction floor to Irish road — every step explained.

01

Choose Your Car

Browse our Arbitrage database or tell us what you're looking for. We track prices across Japanese auctions and retail sites daily.

02

We Source & Bid

Our team in Japan inspects vehicles, reviews auction sheets (grade, mileage, condition), and bids on your behalf at USS, JAA, TAA, or ARAI auctions.

03

Shipping & Customs

We arrange RoRo or container shipping from Japan to Ireland. CIF (Cost + Insurance + Freight) is included in your quote.

04

Irish Taxes & Registration

We calculate and pay all Irish taxes upfront: Customs Duty (10%), VAT (23%), VRT (CO₂-based), and NOx levy. No surprises.

05

Delivery

Your car is registered, NCT'd, and delivered — ready for Irish roads. Dealerships get priority handling.
